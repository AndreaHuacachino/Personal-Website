Introduction

Hi, I am Andrea!


I am a 1st year Biochemistry and Biophysics PhD student at PSOM at the University of Pennsylvania. Born in Peru, I have strong ties to my heritage. Speaking 3 languages, English Spanish and Quechua, I find the most important aspect of life to be the ability to connect with others. This is why I create art as well as conduct biomedical research, to help others.
